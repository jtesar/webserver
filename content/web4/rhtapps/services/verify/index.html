Red Hat Certification Central Verify a Red Hat Certified Professional

Get immediate verification of a person's Red Hat certificate by entering the certificate number below.

Format: ###-###-###

The value of Red Hat certifications is demonstrated by validating your ability to take on the most ambitious IT projects. Red Hat Certified Professionals have shown they are skilled, proven and ready by passing one or more hands-on, practical exams. Prospective employers, clients, managers, and others can verify someone's Red Hat certification status by entering the person's Red Hat certification ID above.

Note that Red Hat only verifies certifications that are considered current.

If you are a Red Hat Certified Professional and wish for your current certifications to be available for verification, make sure you have created a personal user account that is mapped to your certification ID.
